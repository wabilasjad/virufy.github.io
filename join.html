Who We Need

Clinical Outreach

Big data from patients of multiple cultures and backgrounds is necessary for our AI algorithms to learn and be applicable globally. We need your help to reach out to hospitals across the world where many patients are tested positive for COVID-19 to conduct clinical data collection studies.

Grant Writer

Seeking individuals to support us in researching, writing and coordinating the grant application process and keeping records of donors.

Clinical Research Coordinator (CRC)

Seeking individuals to support us in coordinating clinical trial activities. The CRC will be involved in preparing research protocols, reporting, informed consent, conduct of research, safeguarding PHI, and any general administrative matters.

AI Engineers and Mentors

Our team of students works with machine learning, deep learning, and signal analysis for audio, video, and textual analysis in a medical setting. We welcome students with experience in these domains and mentors with deep expertise that can guide us to build a robust solution as soon as possible.

Social Media Manager

We're in need of students who can help to manage our social media platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram). Responsibilities include following trending topics related to Virufy, sharing relevant articles on our platforms, and writing blog content. High English proficiency and ability to write concisely is needed.
